Airbus Helicopters (previously previously known as Eurocopter) is the world’s leading manufacturer of helicopters, offering the most comprehensive products of civilian and military helicopters in the world.

Airbus Helicopters is a part of Airbus Group which is a global aerospace and defense leader. Airbus Helicopters employs approximately 23,000 people worldwide. Its fleet in service includes 12,000 helicopters operated by more than 3,000 customers across 150 countries. Worldwide, the company maintains a 50-percent market share in the civil and parapublic markets. Airbus is a leader in innovation and improvement in helicopter aviation.

Airbus has decided to secure its position in Turkey in 2023 by nominating a distributor. Airbridge shall represent civil segment of Airbus Helicopters for sales and after sales support. By securing all the values that makes Airbus a world leader, Airbridge will serve in Turkish market with its maintenance, technical and operational capability.
